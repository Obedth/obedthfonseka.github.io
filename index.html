About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated audio engineering student with six years of experience as a pianist and a solid foundation in music composition, audio production, and post-production techniques. Skilled in Cubase and experienced in live and studio settings, I excel at transforming creative ideas into professional-quality sound. My strong problem-solving abilities, leadership experience in school and church music programs, and commitment to continuous learning make me a valuable asset to any team seeking excellence in music production and audio engineering.
